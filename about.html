ABOUT US

Jamalpur is a city in Munger district (Surrounded by River Ganga from Three Side, this is the second City after Varanasi of this kind) in the Indian state of Bihar. The name Jamalpur literally means Jamal (beautiful) pur (town). Jamalpur is best known for its large railway workshop in the Eastern Railway Zone (India) employing over 25,000 people at one time. The Jamalpur Workshop was the first full-fledged railway workshop facilities in India which was set up on 8 Feb 1862 by the East Indian Railway. There is the oldest institute of Briticisms at the railway institute“The Indian Railways Institute of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering” (IRIMEE). The town was established during the British Raj with the Railway Institute forming the cultural hub. Jamalpur is located at Raj Mahal hills 25.31°N 86.49°E at an average elevation of 151 metres (495 ft).The city is in the Munger district which is 8.5 kilometres (5.3 mi) North West of Jamalpur.

DIESEL SHED, JAMALPUR
BRIEF HISTORY

Inaugurated on 12th December 1991, Diesel shed, Jamalpur. The shed boasts of 54 B.G. locomotives catering to the traffic requirements of Eastern Railway, East Central Railway and North Frontier Railway. The shed has also taken up the maintenance of DEMUs, introduced for the first time in the erstwhile Eastern Railway and is the first Diesel Shed of Indian Railways to do so.

This shed won the National Award in National level for the year 2011 – 12. With all performance parameters, including the most important ones like Outage, Ineffective percentage and Reliability, showing tremendous improvement and stabilizing rapidly at the traditional high performance level of Eastern Railway, the shed is all set to become one of the leading diesel sheds in the coming years, specializing in both the fields of Diesel Loco maintenance as well as DEMU maintenance.

ATHELETICS

Indian railway which is the largest organization and economic backbone of India is constantly progressing towards golden future from its glorious past. Eastern Railway Diesel Shed Jamalpur which is an integral part of Indian railways is situated on the spiritual land of Jamalpur within the circumference of 3km in the beautiful valley of Kali Pahadi.

Diesel Shed Jamalpur has been organizing Open Athletics Championship every year since 2004 in addition to the decided responsibility.

Athletes from district level, state level, national level and International level participate in this 3 day event. Moreover, students from various schools of the district and nearby districts and states participate in this championship. Almost 70 events with almost 2000 athletes in various age-groups of boys, girls, men and women are organized and cash prizes, gifts, medals and certificates are awarded to the winner in each age group. Moreover, Best Athlete Award, Championship award (Champion district and Champion school who win most medals) is also awarded.

In previous championships, we had the privilege to have following Officers are dignitarieshad also presided over the above championship as Chief Guest and Special Guest and awarded prize to winners at different occasions since the year’2004.
